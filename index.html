Personal Network

Tribes is designed to be personal. There are no online profiles. You can only connect via scanning a QR code.

Connect via Interests

Build your profile of interest by choosing from 20 topics. You can assign a topic to a post to reach all your friends that are interested.

Finite Inbox

Tribes is not designed to make you stay forever. Only active posts and messages are in your inbox.

Explore your Archive

Archive interesting conversations and find them via advanced search.


In a classical social network you have an online profile to represent your online identity. This design is intuitive but it comes with unnatural consequences. 1. You exist at two points at the same time. 2. The online you can be found and looked at by other people. 3. It encourages self-presentation. All of these are not desirable.

Thus Tribes has no online profiles. Other users only see your name and your picture. There is no search for users. The only way to connect is via a QR-Code that changes everytime you make a connection with someone.
